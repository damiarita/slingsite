# Slingsite: Terms of Service in Plain English

Welcome to Slingsite! By using Slingsite, you agree to our [official legal terms](/en/terms-and-conditions/).

But here’s the deal in simple terms:

## Your Files Stay Yours & Private

**Your files never leave your device**. All compression happens locally in your browser. We don't upload or store your files, and you keep 100% of the rights to your content.

When you use the Google Drive button to upload images, a popup may appear asking for permission to access your name, email, and profile picture. This information is used only in your browser to connect to your Google Drive and is never sent to any server—your personal details remain securely in your browser.
